Characters I Play And How Much My Friends Hate Them.



Nearly all of my friends have one big thing in common while we are playing a fighting game: They hate the characters I pick. So, to commemorate that, here's a tierlist of how much each character makes them rage and scream at me. Note: This list includes characters from multiple games, not all of which I main or am particularly good at, but all of them make my friends have no fun.


Tiers Characters Game

#2 Hakumen BBCF
#3 Nines The Phantom BBCF
#4 Azrael BBCF
#5 Slayer GGXX+R
